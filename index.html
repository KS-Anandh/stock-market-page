Workshop Curriculum

A 3-hour beginner-friendly workshop broken into 7 interactive modules.

Module 1: What is Money & Why Invest?

Understand the basics of money, savings vs investments, and why wealth creation matters.

Module 2: Indian Financial Ecosystem

Learn about SEBI, NSE, BSE, RBI, and how the stock market fits into our economy.

Module 3: What is a Stock?

Simple explanation of stocks with real-life company examples, IPOs, and listings.

Module 4: How Prices Change?

Understand supply & demand, market news impact, and investor psychology.

Module 5: Brokers & Accounts

Learn about Demat & Trading accounts, step-by-step opening guide, and broker types.

Module 6: Market Basics

Types of orders, market timings, settlement cycles (T+1 explained simply).

Module 7: First Look at Trading

Introduction to trading platforms, watchlists, order books, and basic charts.
